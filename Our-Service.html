We locate the manufacturing unit depending on the customers’ expectations and requirement with regard to the quality, quantity, fashion and price. Most importantly, we aim to maximize customer value and satisfaction. We pride ourselves in sourcing the highest quality fabrics, as that is the key to ensure smooth and superior quality production.

System Of Ordering

The way you can buy products from us

  1. On receipt of inquiry, we immediately make samples to determine cost and inform buyer
  2. Upon acceptance of price and delivery, Buyer issues purchase order.
  3. We provide Pro forma invoice for opening of L/C by buyer.
  4. Once L/C is received, it is transferred to the Factories, who open back-to-back L/C for Yarn, Fabric, Accessories etc.
  5. We stay in constant touch with the Factories regarding shipment of raw materials and keep the Buyer updated.
  6. As production begins, our QA Team vigilantly monitors the production to ensure that All Buyer requirements are completely followed. We also keep the Buyer apprised of any issues.
  7. Goods are made ready for shipments only after the final inspection is made.

Payment and delevery system
