About Our Company

"MERCHANT TEX” vision is to provide our international buyers, resourceful insights of the untapped Bangladesh market, delivering the right quality and timely shipment with maximum customer satisfaction. Our mission is to invite international buyers to Bangladesh and make buying from Bangladesh a pleasant experience and profitable for our clients.

To provide our clients with a minimal turnaround time and quality products all our associate factories are equipped with hi-tech machineries and highly skilled employees. Both Woven and Knit suppliers are system driven and meet universally accepted compliance standards.

We guarantee authentic brands avoiding inferior commercial imitations, or even dangerous counterfeits. Thanks to our experiences and the knowledge of the market, we are consequently able to give a priority access to special, highly valuable commercial conditions and opportunities.

We believe in the professionalism confidence as the start of a good commercial relationship. That is why we guarantee professionalism and the maximum discretion, from business meetings until the contract conclusion. Moreover we guarantee the respect of the industrial secret.

We represent a significant opportunity for the growth of small and medium size companies: Together we plan every kind of marketing strategy, suggesting the best solutions for achieving pre-set goals, even the most ambitious ones. We assist in obtaining exclusive licenses for Italian or patented products. 

We are committed to: 
  • Over 40 years of driving brand innovations for clients and customer
  • The best Product Development & Sourcing
  • On time production & timely deliveries
  • Quality assurance & Inspection
  • Most competitive prices for the best possible quality.
  • Communication & Coordination
  • Shipment & Tracking
  • A headache & hassle free one stop solution to Customers.
