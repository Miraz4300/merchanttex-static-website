Production Units

We take great care in selecting the vendors who manufacture our products. Most of the production units are Compliance certified or are Government Recognized Export House.

Our production units have following like: 

  • Comprehensive in-house production set up.
  • Facility for design and pattern making.
  • In-house stitching / Finishing / Packing facilities.
  • Equipped with washing plants,
  • Sound raw material / fabric sourcing facility.
  • Set up for embroidery work / patch work / appliqué work and other value additions.
  • Ethical statement (Child labor, Wage & Benefit, Hours of work, Health and safety, Harassment, Woman’s Rights).
