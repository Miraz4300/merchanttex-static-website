Non woven bag

We have vast experience to execute Non woven Bag. We do supply Non woven Bag in USA, CANADA &amp; AUSTRALIA. TYPES OF NON-WOVEN BAG:

D CUT NON WOVEN BAGS

D cut non-woven bags that can be used to carry bags which is a great replacement for plastic bags at shop and stores. The Handle of D cut bag is soft and hence it is an essential part of the bag, they do not get ripped that easily.

W CUT NON WOVEN BAGS

W cut or U cut non-woven bags are quite commonly known as grocery bags. The cut type is similar to that of plastic bags that one gets when buying groceries. They are a good replacement for harmful plastic bags that are doing a lot of harm to our environment.

LOOP HANDLE NON WOVEN BAGS

Loop handle non-woven bags fill multi needs besides passing on an upscale look. The expandable base gusset and the delicate Loop handles, one on each side of the bag, keep substance anchored amid movement. Use this bag when you want to increase stability, and it is reducing the likelihood of breakage.
